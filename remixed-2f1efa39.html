08 · Responsible AI

Three real risks. Three real answers. No hand-waving.

EQUIL is a behavioral mirror, not a nanny. It informs and adds friction — it never blocks a transaction or makes a value judgment about your money.

⚠ Risk · Manipulation

Could it exploit emotional states?

Architectural mitigation

The product never serves advertisements. No third party can ever reach users in an emotional state.

Commercial safeguard

Zero tolerance for behavioral exploitation, under strict consumer-protection norms.

⚠ Risk · Privacy

Where does the inference happen?

Architectural mitigation

All inference is strictly on-device or inside the RBI-regulated Account Aggregator framework.

Commercial safeguard

Zero raw cycle data on servers. Ephemeral scores. Instant profile deletion.

⚠ Risk · Paternalism

Who controls the money?

Architectural mitigation

Synthetic friction is introduced only during high-risk psychological windows — never a block.

Commercial safeguard

The user holds ultimate authority. EQUIL makes zero value judgments.

Core principle 01

Absolute user autonomy

The AI informs and adds friction. It is constrained to never block a transaction outright.

Core principle 02

Transparency by design

Algorithmic transparency is absolute. The prompt structure and feed are user-editable.

Core principle 03

Total data sovereignty

Zero external monetization. Behavioral data is never sold, licensed, or used for ad targeting.
